How it works

Lock the money in escrow. Verify the outcome. Release it only if the work is real.

01 — Lock

Escrow, capped on-chain

The agent locks funds. The contract refuses anything over its budget cap. Overspending is impossible.

02 — Verify

A machine decides

The outcome is a fact, not an opinion — here: the CVEs are gone and the tests pass.

03 — Settle or refund

The chain pays out

Verified → the provider gets paid. Failed → the money goes back. Not the agent's call.
